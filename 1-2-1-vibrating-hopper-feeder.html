    Vibrating Hopper Feeder

    Producing fiber batt being fed to carding machine

    Vibrating hopper feeder, as important nonwoven machinery, plays a key role in forming and providing a uniform fiber batt to the carding machine.
    In the nonwoven manufacturing process, the vibrating card feeding unit not only aids in the storage of fibers and fiber batts but also ensures the uniformity of the fiber mixture, thereby improving production efficiency and product quality.

    Working width: 1400mm, 1700mm, 1850mm, 2150mm, 2350mm
    Capacity: 200-400kg/h
    Request Information
    Functions
    • Volumetric feeding is ensured through vibration assistance and photoelectric control.
    • The vibrating hopper machine plays a crucial role in further blending the fibers and ensuring a uniform fiber batt for the subsequent carding process by utilizing a vibration leveling device.
    Features
    • Fiber storage volume is controlled by multiple photoelectric controls, which is adjustable as needed.
    • The inner wall is specially treated to be smooth and burr-free, preventing fiber accumulation and rust.
    • Adjustable vibration amplitude meets different production requirements.
    • High-level chute feeder and frequency feeding control ensure uniform fiber batts.
