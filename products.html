    Feeding & Carding

    The feeding and carding process is one of the most critical steps in a nonwoven production line, as the uniformity directly affects the quality of the final product. Carding involves separating tangled and curled fiber bundles into individual fibers, straightening them out in parallel, and collecting them to form a uniform thin fiber web for output.

    Needle Punching

    Needle punching technology is the mechanical method to reinforce fiber webs. The needle punching machine utilizes needles with barbs to penetrate the fluffy fiber web repeatedly, compressing the fibers while making them entangle with each other. This process creates a cohesive force, converting the fibers into a nonwoven felt product with a certain thickness and strength.
