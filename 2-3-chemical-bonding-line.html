    Chemical Bonding Line

    Complete production line for chemical bonded nonwoven
    • Spray Bonded Wadding Production Line

      Chemical bonding technology is primarily employed to produce nonwoven materials with a three-dimensional network structure, characterized by fluffy texture.

      Production width: 2000mm - 3200mm
      Design capacity: 200-500kg/h
    • Scouring Pad Production Line

      Scouring pad production line utilizes a combination of chemical bonding and hot air drying processes to produce cleaning cloth products.

      Production width: 1000mm - 2200mm
      Product weight: 100gsm ~800gsm
    • Chemical Steeping and Bonding Production Line

      Chemical steeping and bonding production line processes synthetic fiber raw materials through opening, blending, carding, web forming, further carding, saturation impregnation, drying and shaping, and winding to produce rolls of fabric.

      Production width: 1500mm - 2500mm
      Product weight: 20gsm ~80gsm
