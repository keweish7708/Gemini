    Company profile

    Sail Nonwoven Machinery Co., Ltd. (SAIL NONWOVEN MACHINERY) is a leading manufacturer of nonwoven machinery in China. With over 10 years of experience in the field, our company specializes in manufacturing nonwoven equipment and providing comprehensive nonwoven production solutions, supporting production equipment, and excellent services for nonwoven fabric manufacturers worldwide. The produced equipment covers various nonwoven production line processes, with needle punching, thermal bonding, and chemical bonding being the most advantageous categories.
