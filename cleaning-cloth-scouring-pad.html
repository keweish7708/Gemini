    Background

    Non-woven materials, by virtue of their unique three-dimensional mesh and porous structure, possess certain strength while also exhibiting good moisture absorption, durability, and antimicrobial properties. They can be applied in various cleaning and scouring material fabrics. In addition to traditional non-woven cloths produced by needle punching, other processes have been combined to develop products such as scouring pad, stitched wipes, and towel fabrics.

    How we can assist cleaning cloth & scouring pads manufacturers

    Our specialized non-woven equipment can effectively meet the production needs of customers for cleaning cloth and scouring pad, with high production efficiency and stable quality. We provide rationalized non-woven production solutions for the production of different types of non-woven cleaning products, along with supporting non-woven production equipment, to ensure peace of mind for our customers.
