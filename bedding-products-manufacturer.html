    Nonwoven Production Solution for Bedding Products Manufacturer in Morocco

    The customer is a local nonwoven fabric manufacturing enterprise specializing in various bedding products, with strong capabilities. In order to meet market demand and develop environmentally friendly and high-quality mattress materials, they sought reliable and cost-effective nonwoven production solutions. Our company tailored a nonwoven production line to meet their diverse needs, capable of producing products such as thermal bonded wadding, thermal bonded padding, etc.

    To address the customer's urgent demand for nonwoven fabric in the market, our company expedited the manufacturing and installation of the nonwoven production line. Following successful testing in one go, the production line was able to produce a variety of nonwoven products. This provided effective assistance to the customer in swiftly capturing the nonwoven fabric market and facilitated the export of nonwoven fabric products to European and American markets.
