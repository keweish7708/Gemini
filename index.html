    Applications

    Nonwovens involve in diversity of technical and functional applications

    Carpet

    Nonwoven Production Line Solution for Carpet

    Nonwoven carpet production mainly utilizes needle punching technology, which offers a simpler production process compared to traditional woven and tufted carpets, resulting in higher efficiency and larger output.

    Geotextile

    Nonwoven Production Line Solution for Geotextile

    Geotextile products play corresponding roles in engineering and construction projects, such as reinforcement, drainage, filtration, anti-filtering, isolation, etc. These products mainly include woven geotextiles, non-woven geotextiles, and other geotechnical synthetic composite materials.

    Filter Fabric

    Nonwoven Production Line Solution for Filter Fabric

    Nonwoven filter fabrics mainly cover applications such as air purification and personal protective materials, industrial smoke filtration, liquid filtration, etc.

    Automotive Interior Fabric

    Nonwoven Production Line Solution for Automotive Interior Fabric

    Nonwoven materials are widely used in the automotive industry due to their unique characteristics such as lightweight, sound absorption, impact resistance, and ease of molding.

    Nonwoven Fabric Substrates

    Nonwoven Production Line Solution for Nonwoven Fabric Substrates

    Nonwoven materials, due to their unique three-dimensional mesh structure, can organically combine with various polymers or other materials.
